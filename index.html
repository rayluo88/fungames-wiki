Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating enemies and collecting power-ups.

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and characters to help you combat the growing monster threat. Each run is unique, offering a different experience every time you play.

With its addictive gameplay and simple controls, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive? Challenge yourself and try to beat your high score!

Play for free directly in your browser - no downloads or installations required. Compatible with both desktop and mobile devices.

How to Play

1

Move Your Character

Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate around the map and avoid incoming monsters.

2

Attack Enemies

Your character attacks automatically. Position yourself strategically to maximize damage.

3

Collect Power-ups

Defeat monsters to collect experience and power-ups that make you stronger.
